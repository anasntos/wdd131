About Seattle

Seattle is a vibrant city in the northwestern United States, located in the state of Washington. Known for its stunning natural scenery between mountains and water, it is famous for the Space Needle, its strong tech industry with companies like Amazon and Microsoft, and its rich cultural and musical scene. Seattle is also known for its rainy weather, cozy coffee shops, and innovative spirit, making it an important economic and cultural hub in the region.
